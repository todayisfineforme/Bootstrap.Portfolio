About

Born in Dayton, Ohio. Moved to Chicago in the fall of 2014. Since then has been involved in the local comedy scene in one way or another. Improv, Sketch, Stand-up, Storytelling, Producing, you name it. Graduated from the IO school of improv in 2016, then from CIC in 2017, and then rounding it out by graduatring from Second City's conservatory program in 2019.

After deciding to stop fighting his own destiny, he's picking up coding to round out his skillsets.
